Scribe is a framework for crowdsourcing the transcription of text-based documents

In other words, we've created a codebase to scaffold transcription projects so you can focus on the important work of configuring your project to your materials and communities. Designed for materials containing rich metadata in fields fairly uniform across documents and informed by previous projects by NYPL Labs and Zooniverse, we've broken transcription efforts into a series of discrete tasks. Users of these projects will mark fields, transcribe text, and perform quality control to verify transcription accuracy. Like every element in Scribe, these tasks are optional and configurable. Scribe is not a project in a box, but it establishes the foundation for a developer to finish building and configuring the project.  Read more...
